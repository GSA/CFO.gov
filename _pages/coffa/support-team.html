ABOUT US

We are a dynamic team of problem solvers providing cross-functional support to the COFFA and the related initiatives. We deliver streamlined meeting management, communications, resources, cross-council coordination, and project and program management support.

FOCUS AND FUNCTION

Administrative Functions in Support of the COFFA Council

  • Logistics. Work to ensure COFFA Council members can focus on the objectives instead of the meeting logistics.
  • Communications. Develop well-crafted messaging and materials to support the various COFFA Council initiatives and projects.
  • Resources. Provide access to a wide range of data collection and meeting support tools for the COFFA Council meetings and working groups.

Coordination Across Government

  • Cross-council coordination. Connect with other government-wide councils to expand our knowledge base and extend our reach.
  • Cross-agency connections. Coordinate engagement with government agencies on COFFA Council topics.

Initiative and Project Management in Support of the COFFA Council Priorities

  • Outreach. Share and distribute summaries and outputs for COFFA Council projects and initiatives.
  • Meeting support. Oversee logistics for working group meetings for select COFFA Council projects and initiatives.
  • Project Management. Support the COFFA Council discussions and implementation plans for select COFFA Council projects and initiatives with project plans, documentation, and maintenance of outputs.

If you are interested in learning more about the Chief Financial Officers Council or discussing our resources, please reach out to us at COFFA.support@gsa.gov!
