Events

JFMIP sponsors an annual conference and periodic webinar events. These events provide the federal financial management community with relevant and timely information in topics such as government accounting, internal controls, and cybersecurity delivered by the top leaders in government. They also provide a forum for those in the federal financial management community to learn about current issues, exchange knowledge, and share experiences in improving financial management operations and policies. Suggestions for topics, speakers, and volunteers related to JFMIP events can be provided to JFMIPCPE@gao.gov. Below is a listing of upcoming JFMIP events.
