--- title: About the FRPC layout: frpc-default permalink: /frpc/ description: Learn about FRPC and guidance on reporting their real property each year. keyword: CFO Council, FRPC, CFOC, Federal Real Property Council description1: The Federal Real Property Council (FRPC) is an interagency council established by Executive Order 13327, "Federal Real Property Asset Management," and later enacted into law by the Federal Property Management Reform Act (P.L. 114-318) to promote the efficient and economical use of America's real property assets and to assure management accountability for implementing federal real property reforms. The FRPC provides guidance to agencies on reporting their real property each year. ---
