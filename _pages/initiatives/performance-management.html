Performance Management

It is a challenge to show that Federal dollars are spent wisely and that those dollars have the intended impact and produced value to the taxpayer. This strategy aims to tell the American people the value for the funds spent on Financial assistance. It is a long-term strategy focused on demonstrating grant program results by assessing the landscape of current performance practices, promoting a common understanding of performance practices and tools, proposing revisions to guidance, developing and implementing a change management strategy, launching a national dialogue, and producing a centralized location for performance data for consumers.
