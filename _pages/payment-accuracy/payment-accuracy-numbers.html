Annual Improper Payments Dashboard

The Payment Integrity Information Act of 2019 requires an annual publication to fulfill multiple statutory reporting requirements for both the agency and OMB. The interactive dashboard below lets you explore and compare payment integrity data through visualizations. The accompanying blog provides additional context to the government wide rate.
