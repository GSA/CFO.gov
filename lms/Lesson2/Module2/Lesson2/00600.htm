What is Substantial Involvement?

The criteria for distinguishing between grants and cooperative agreements is that for cooperative agreements, substantial involvement expected between the agency and the recipient DURING performance of the programmatic work activities.

Substantial involvement is collaboration between the awarding agency and recipient in the management and/or performance of a project, as outlined in the terms and conditions of the award. Typically the awarding agency is providing programmatic support. Support may include technical expertise, access to specialized equipment, labor, etc. Substantial involvement should not be used a means of exercising control over the recipient.
